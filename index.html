Challenges Faced

⚠Process & Communication

  • ~50% of employees do not raise tickets — rely on verbal communication, causing delays or tasks being skipped entirely
  • Defined SOPs are not being followed consistently across departments

⚠External Dependencies

  • Unable to meet TAT due to heavy reliance on external vendors for critical resolutions

⚠Hardware Issues

  • Outdated hardware models leading to increased maintenance costs and repeated breakdowns

⚠Out-of-Scope Workload

  • IT department burdened with non-IT tasks from other departments:
  • → Payslip generation
  • → Certificate generation
  • → Election work
  • → Excel formula support
  • → Bulk mail operations

"These challenges directly impact our ability to scale and deliver on time."

Learnings & Improvements

Key Learnings

  • Automation is critical for scaling
  • SOPs & documentation save time
  • User awareness reduces IT workload
  • Proactive monitoring prevents fires
  • Verbal communication leads to missed tasks — ticketing discipline is non-negotiable

Actionable Improvements

  • Implement automated onboarding/offboarding
  • Integrate monitoring & alerting tools
  • Improve response time via strict SLAs
  • Better asset tracking and management
  • Conduct regular IT training for staff
  • Enforce mandatory ticket-raising policy
  • Define and communicate IT scope boundaries
